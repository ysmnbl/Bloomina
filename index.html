Our Story

One ordinary day, I was walking when I passed by a florist — the colors, the scent, the elegance of the flowers completely caught my heart. I thought to myself: “Why not turn this beauty into something bigger?” As a computer science student, the idea of combining my love for flowers with my skills felt just right. That’s when Bloomina was born — through a class project, but also through passion. What started as a school assignment became the seed of a dream: to share beautiful blooms with everyone, online.
